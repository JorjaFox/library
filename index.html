Jorja Fox
Biographies • Filmography • Advocacy • Interviews • News Articles

Best known for her role of Sara Sidle in the long running CBS TV series, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Jorja also leads Seafox Productions, a stage and film production company, with which she has also produced a musical, Stay Forever: The Life and Music of Dusty Springfield and continued her involvement with politics and advocacy.

Jorja currently appears on CSI: Crime Scene Investigation as a part-time main cast member, while working with Animal Defenders International and the Humane Society of the United States for animal rights.
