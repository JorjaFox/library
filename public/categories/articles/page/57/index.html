  2. favorite destinations?

  4. currently reading?
    A people’s history of the US by Howard Zinn (again) and Water For Elephants by Sara Gruen
  5. favorite design found in nature?
    rain forests.
  6. 3 things you can’t live without?
    laughter, adventure, and music .
  7. favorite beverage?

    the Beatles, MLK, Robert Redford, and Maya Angelou.

    Heal the Bay, NRDC, FINCA, PETA, Animal Acres, Human Rights Campaign, HSUS
